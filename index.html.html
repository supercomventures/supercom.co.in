Step-by-step setup guides for all major website builders.

The gold standard for dropshipping. Fast setup, powerful apps, and built-in payment processing.

Drag-and-drop simplicity. Best for visual stores and creative products. No coding needed.

  • Start with Wix eCommerce plan
  • Use ADI wizard for instant setup

Maximum control and flexibility. Best for those who want zero platform fees at scale.

  • Set up Razorpay gateway plugin

Module 02 — Market

Master Meta
Business Suite

Facebook & Instagram ads are the #1 driver of dropshipping revenue. Full setup guide inside.

🏢
Set up your Business Account
Go to business.facebook.com → Create Account. Add your Facebook Page, Instagram account, and store domain. Verify your domain under Business Settings → Brand Safety.
🎯
Install the Meta Pixel
Create a Pixel in Events Manager → Copy the Pixel ID → Paste into Shopify under Preferences. This tracks every visitor and purchase on your store automatically.
📣
Create your first Ad Campaign
In Ads Manager, choose Conversions objective → Create an Ad Set targeting your niche → Upload your product video/image → Write a scroll-stopping headline and CTA.
📊
Read your Ad Analytics
Monitor CPM, CTR, CPC, ROAS daily. Kill ads with CTR below 1%. Scale winners by duplicating the ad set with 20% higher budget every 3 days. Never edit a running ad.
🔁
Retargeting & Lookalike Audiences
Create a Custom Audience of website visitors (last 30 days), then build a 1–2% Lookalike from your buyers. Retarget cart abandoners with a discount within 24 hours.
Pro Ad Tips
01
Budget your test correctlySpend ₹400–800/day per ad set for 3–5 days before judging results. Need at least 50 conversions/week to exit the learning phase.
02
Use video over imagesVideo ads get 3× more engagement. Even a 15-second product demo filmed on your phone outperforms polished static images.
03
Audience stackingLayer interests with behaviors (e.g. "online shoppers" + niche interest) to narrow to high-intent buyers and cut wasted spend.
04
Schedule ads for peak hoursRun ads between 6pm–11pm in your target timezone. Use ad scheduling to avoid burning budget on low-traffic hours.
05
Test 3 creatives minimumLaunch every campaign with at least 3 different creatives per ad set. The algorithm finds the winner — your job is to feed it options.
